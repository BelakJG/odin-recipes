Roasted Golden Potatoes

Description

These Roasted Yukon Gold Potatoes are true veggie perfection. Perfectly tender, easy to make, and flavorful thanks to a simple (but delicious) seasoning blend. This recipe takes a basic veggie side dish and makes it something special.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes (~3 potatoes)
  • 1.5 tablespoons Olive oil
  • .5 teaspoon salt
  • .5 teaspoon garlic powder
  • .5 teaspoon dried oregano
  • .5 teaspoon dried thyme
  • black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°F, prepare baking tray with parchment paper
  2. Cut potatoes to about 1 inch chunks
  3. places chunks into a large bowl, add seasonings and oil
  4. Toss potatoes to coat evenly in oil and seasonings
  5. spread onto prepared baking tray
  6. roast in oven for 25-30 minutes
  7. Let cool, and enjoy
