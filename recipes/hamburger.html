Hamburger Recipe

Description

These homemade burgers offer a rich and meaty taste, with a punch of zesty herbs that can be customized with your preferred flavors. A few simple key ingredients help to consistently create juicy hamburger patties, no matter which cooking method you prefer.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground chuck
  • 0.5 cup crushed Panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • .5 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet to 350-400 degrees F.
  2. Set out a large mixing bowl. Add in all ingredients. Mix by hand until the meat mixture is smooth, but stop once the mixture looks even.
  3. Divide mixture into 6 even portions. Pressing it flat into the bowl and using a knife may make this easier.
  4. Press these portion into patty shapes slightly larger than the buns you're using are to account for shrinkage. To avoid the middle puffing up you can use a spoon or your thumb to press a dent into the middle of each patty.
  5. Grill or fry patties for 3-4 minutes on each side.
  6. Stack burgers on buns and add the toppings of your choice.
